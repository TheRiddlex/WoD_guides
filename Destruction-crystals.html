Obtaining crystals


To begin with, to obtain crystals we need an item with durability and a scroll of destruction.

Using the sroll of destruction leads to the destruction of the thing and the creation of a crystallized magical ether - crystals. In this case, the color of the resulting crystal depends on the color of the thing, and their probability of obtaining - from the strength of the thing and from your luck.

You can purchase this scroll in the Professional Guilds of Craftsmen 's shops in the City Squares, Witch' s Warden at Kayar Ridge, Sevenval 's Shop in Maartro Village, the cost of the scroll is 10. After purchasing the scroll, you can use its magic to destroy things to obtain crystals. It should be noted that the thing you want to destroy at the time of use should be in a backpack. After clicking on the scroll, a dialog box will appear in which you will have to select the object on which you want to use the scroll of destruction.

After pressing the "Execute" button, the target item and the scroll will disappear from the backpack, but a single crystal will appear, the color of which depends on the color of the thing being destroyed, and the size of the level of the thing to be destroyed and your luck. Also in the chat you will receive a message that will inform you about which crystal was received and what thing was destroyed for this:

As indicated above - the higher the strength, the greater the probability of obtaining a crystal. That is, in order to get a crystal with a hundred percent probability you have to destroy a new thing. With each fixing of the thing this probability decreases. It is worth noting the fact that the probability is affected by the second value, that is, a thing with a strength of 0/34 has a higher probability of crystal dropping than a thing with a strength of 33/33. The probability of obtaining a crystal is directly proportional to strength. If the destruction fails, you will get a window like this:

This window will also be accompanied by an inscription in the chat:

It is worth noting that in case of unsuccessful destruction the scroll of destruction from the backpack does not disappear , but the thing as written above will disappear irrevocably.

When destroying things, the following rule should be taken into account: when a thing is destroyed, you get a crystal level corresponding to the level of the destroyed thing, or, with a small chance, a crystal level higher. This means that from the 1-5 level things will usually be small crystals (with a small chance - medium), from things 6-10 levels - medium crystals (with a small chance big), from things 11-15 - large ( with a small chance of massive), of things 16-20 - always massive crystals. This rule applies to things of all "colors". Destruction of "red" things of any level and style things will not bring any results.

Not all things are prone to destruction on crystals. For example, let's start with the fact that any things without strength simply will not be displayed in the list of things under destruction. The exception is Unaryus' negatives and the armor of the underwater suit. Although they do not have strength, but the list displays like things with a strength of 0/0. Destruction of such things will always lead to failure - and things will just disappear so that the probability of obtaining a crystal is zero. Also, certain types of things with durability are not subject to destruction, for example: all kinds of rings and amulets, all kinds of backpacks and bags, absolutely any belts, quiver. But at the same time, unlike the quiver, any bows have the property of destruction. Any tools of labor are also subject to this process.

Crystals are flickering , sparkling , shiny , and also shining and "mythical" radiant . A further description of these crystals will be given below.

Flickering crystals

As already mentioned above - crystals in addition to color differ in their shape. In this paragraph we will describe: a massive shimmering crystal, a large shimmering crystal, a shimmering crystal and a small shimmering crystal.

To obtain these crystals, you can destroy any thing with the strength of a set of ordinary things, gray bows and tools .

These crystals are used mainly in the profession of the jeweler, where they act as ingredients for various green runes: a
small shimmering crystal - in the recipes for creating the green runes of the Jeweler by skill +0 ... + 60;
shimmering crystal - in the recipes of creating the green runes of the Jeweler by skill +90 ... + 210;
a large shimmering crystal - in the recipes of the creation of the green runes of the Jeweler by skill +240 ... + 360;
a massive shimmering crystal - in the recipes of the creation of the green runes of the Jeweler by skill +390 and above.

 

Sparkling crystals

As already mentioned above - crystals in addition to color differ in their shape. In this paragraph we will describe: a massive sparkling crystal, a large sparkling crystal, a sparkling crystal and a small sparkling crystal.

To obtain these crystals, you can destroy any things with strength from sets of quality items, green bows and tools .

These crystals are used in a variety of quests. In addition to the destruction of things, these crystals can be found in hiding places during the passage of the quest "Book of the Dead". Basically, these crystals are used in the profession of the jeweler, where they act as ingredients for various runes: a
small sparkling crystal - in the recipes for creating blue and green runes of the Jeweler by skill +0 ... + 60;
sparkling crystal - in the recipes for creating blue and green runes Jeweler skill +90 ... + 210;
a large sparkling crystal - in the recipes for creating blue and green runes Jeweler skill +240 ... + 360;
a massive sparkling crystal - in the recipes of the creation of blue and green runes of the Jeweler by skill +390 and above.

 

4. Brilliant crystals.

As already mentioned above - crystals in addition to color differ in their shape. This paragraph will describe: a massive shiny crystal, a large shiny crystal, a shiny crystal and a small shiny crystal.

To obtain these crystals, you can destroy any thing with the strength of a set of rare items, blue bows and tools .

In addition to the destruction of things, these crystals can be found in precious boxes on an event. Brilliant crystals are actively used by jewelers in the passage of quests, the production of blue runes. Jewelers: a
small shiny crystal - in recipes for creating blue runes of the Jeweler skill +0 ... + 60;
brilliant crystal - in the recipes of the creation of the blue runes of the Jeweler by skill +90 ... + 210;
a large shiny crystal - in the recipes of the creation of the blue runes of the Jeweler by skill +240 ... + 360;
a massive shiny crystal - in the recipes of creating the blue runes of the Jeweler by skill +390 and above.

 

5. Shining crystals.

As already mentioned above - crystals in addition to color differ in their shape. In this paragraph we will describe: a massive shining crystal, a large shining crystal, a shining crystal and a small shining crystal.

To obtain these crystals, you can destroy any things with strength from sets of unique items, purple bows and tools .

These crystals, for the time being, have no application in the game.

 

6. Radiant crystals.

In this section, "mythical" red crystals will be described, which can not now be obtained in the game. When using a roll of destruction on any red thing, the scroll disappears, but the crystal does not appear in the backpack. Naturally, these crystals have no application in the game.
