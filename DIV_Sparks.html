Sparks of Heavenly Fire are an alternative way to raise almost any reputation.
Along with the Reputation Points, sparks can be used for Medals of Worship and even Initiation Quests of Underground Knights (From Level 11).


Sparks can be obtained:
- From Trade Fairs
- From Luck Glow Event
- From Precious Chests
- From Trade Journey
- From additional Administration events


After obtaining the Spark of Heavenly fire you need to hand it in to the Corresponding NPC of the reputation you want to increase.

Take in mind that each reputation can have different restrictions and different ratio of reputation for Spark.

Additional Notes:
- Sparks can be used unlimited amount of times to increase any reputation as long as the restrictions are fullfilled.
- Sparks which hit the maximum restriction may as well exceed it, for example using spark on level 7 having 2790 reputation of Destroyers of Chaos, your rep will move to 2990 points.
