Introduction

From level 5 you will gain access to belts.
Belts are just like any other armour, you need to put them on to use them.
Although belts don't add any attributes, instead they give you additional slots for elixirs in combat.

There are 3 groups of belts:
- Belts
- Heavy Belts
- Sashes
You can wear up to 2 belts at the same time,
however you cannot use 2 Heavy belts or 2 Sashes at the same time.

After putting on a belt, you will notice that new slots lighten up.
Now you are able to use more elixirs in fights.
Whenever you put off a belt, the slots will be unequipped and unaccessible to use.

Having more than 6 slots you will unlock next page, where the new slots will appear.
