Introduction

Unlike all other runes, fewer elements are needed to improve the run of the Sid Speed. The use of one element will improve this rune to the next degree. Moreover, you can strengthen any rune Sid - from gray to violet.

To pump the runes of Gora Capacity, you will also need fewer elements, compared to runes in the rest of the armor. Maximum for each degree will be 2, 4, 10, 16 and 25, respectively.

Obtaining and increasing characteristics

Elements for use in runes in armor, except for the elements of runes in shoes, are made by jewelers. Let's consider what characteristics can be strengthened by elements, closer.

The characteristics to which elements affect are the following:

Agility

Strength

Intuition (abbreviated AND) - increases the chance to inflict a physical critical blow.

Protection (abbreviated to З) - increases the chance to block an enemy's physical attack.

Vitality (abbreviated as F) - increases the indicator of Life; Each Life unit increments the maximum life exponent by 1.

Wisdom (abbreviated M) - increases magic damage; 10 units of Wisdom increase the average magician. damage by 1.

Intelligence (abbreviated as In) is a magical characteristic that directly affects the level of a character's mana.

Persistence (abbreviated to St) is a characteristic that reduces the physical and magical damage received from players.
