Crushing things

In addition to scrapping unnecessary armor, the roll of destruction can be broken up into spectral components. To do this, you need to buy a scrap roll in the Professional store in the Guild of Masters in Dartrong's town square and O'Delweiss .

Any armor can be shattered. Exception : temporary armor, sophisticated things, belts, bags, jewelry, quivers, tools.

To apply the crushing scroll, click on "Use", in the window that opens, select the item that you want to crush. Important! The thing should be in the backpack.

The receipt of the corresponding spectral components will notify the message in the chat.


 

The resulting spectral components

Depending on the color and level of the thing, the components will differ.

As you can see in the image above, crushing, for example, armor and leggings will give you turquoise components. Crushing of the helmet - pomegranate components.

The color of the thing to which the scroll is applied depends on whether the resulting components are flickering, sparkling, or shiny. "Gray" things are split into shimmering components, "green" - in sparkling, "blue" - in shining.

From the level of things depends on the type of components received. Things 1-5 levels are split into fragments, 6-10 levels - into fragments, 11-15 levels - to crystals and 16+ - to debris.

Important! The number of spectral components obtained during crushing depends on the total strength of the thing . Things with less strength than their maximum, with destruction bring a smaller number of spectral components, directly proportional to the strength of the thing.

When splitting things of level 1-5, different fragments are obtained . Consider what splinters can be obtained by crushing various things. Important! The things from the silent caches of the Abandoned house and chess strength 30%, respectively, the resulting number of fragments will be 30% of the below.

Crushing things of level 6-10

As a result of applying the crushing scroll to the items from the 6-10 level kits, fragments are obtained . Arsenal and chess things have 100% strength initially. Things from quiet cracks in the Halls of Magish drop out with 30% strength, therefore, the fragments will drop 30% of the number below.

Flicker fragments can be obtained in the form of a drop from monsters, whose level is equal to the level of the fragment.



 

Crushing of things 11-15 level

When crushing things of 11-15 level, crystals are obtained . Since there are no "gray" things for 11-15 levels, you can get the corresponding crystals only from monsters whose level is equal to the level of the crystal. In things from the quiet caches of the Fortress of the Exiles, the strength is 30% of the total, the resulting number of sparkling crystals will also be 30% of the number in the table.



 

Crushing things of level 16-20

The use of crushing scrolls to armor level 16-20 will lead to debris .



 

Application of spectral components

The main area of ​​application of the components obtained is the use by their jewelers to create rune elements . In the recipes of the elements of runes 1-5 level, the spectral components obtained at the crushing of the 1-5 level of things are required. To create elements of runes 6-10 level you need fragments from things of the same level. In this case, for the elements of the runes, for example, in the armor and leggings, the spectral components obtained during the crushing of armor / leggings are needed; for the elements of the runes in the helmet, helmet crushing components are needed, and so on.

Also spectral components of 6-15 levels are used for illumination of the sophisticated armor of the corresponding levels.


* Note : in the recipes of rune elements for 11-15 and 16-20 levels, flickering ("gray") spectral components are not used. In fact, these components do not have application.
